Why this is different now

The FHFA approved FICO 10T and VantageScore 4.0 for Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac. The new models pull in rent, utility, and BNPL payment history.

Your buyer's 12 months of on-time rent — the rent they paid you to find them — now counts toward qualifying. Combined with disputing inaccurate items under FCRA §611 / §623, a denied buyer can be mortgage-ready in 60–90 days.
