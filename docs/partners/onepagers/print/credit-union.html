Why this matters now

The FHFA approved FICO 10T and VantageScore 4.0 for Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac. The new models pull in rent, utility, and BNPL payment history. Member files that don't qualify on classic FICO can become qualifying once alternative-data history is reported and inaccurate items disputed under FCRA §611 / §623.

60–90 days from "decline" to "qualifying" is realistic for a meaningful share of decline volume.
