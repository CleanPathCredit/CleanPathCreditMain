Clean Path Credit
Partner One-Pagers

Click a sheet, then Cmd + P (Mac) or Ctrl + P (Windows) to print or save as PDF.

  • Loan Officer →

    For mortgage loan officers and brokers. RESPA §8 safe-harbor language built in.

  • Real Estate Agent →

    For RE agents and brokers. NAR Code-of-Ethics language built in.

  • Auto Dealer / F&I →

    For F&I managers at dealerships. Explicit "fee never rolled into financing contract" disclaimer.

  • Credit Union →

    For CU member-services / lending VPs. Three deployment options listed.

To add your QR code: save your channel-specific QR PNG into this folder as qr-lo.png, qr-re.png, qr-auto.png, or qr-cu.png. The image will appear automatically. Without one, a dashed placeholder shows.

To print directly to paper: open the sheet, hit Cmd/Ctrl + P, set paper to US Letter Portrait, margins to Default, scaling to 100%, and uncheck "Headers and footers".

To save as PDF: same dialog, choose Save as PDF as the destination.
