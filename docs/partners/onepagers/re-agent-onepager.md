### Why this is different now

The FHFA approved **FICO 10T and VantageScore 4.0** for Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac — the first major mortgage scoring change in 30+ years. The new models pull in **rent, utility, and BNPL payment history**.

Your buyer's 12 months of on-time rent payments — the rent *they paid you to find them* — now counts toward qualifying. Combined with disputing inaccurate or unverifiable items under FCRA §611 / §623, a denied buyer can become **mortgage-ready in 60–90 days**.
