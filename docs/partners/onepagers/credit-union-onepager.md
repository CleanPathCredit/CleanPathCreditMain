### How the partnership works

**Three deployment options — pick whichever fits your member-benefit framework:**

1. **Decline-letter referral.** Add a line to your decline communications mentioning Clean Path Credit as one credit-readiness option (alongside any others you list). The member chooses whether to engage. We never appear as your "preferred" provider unless you choose to.

2. **Member-portal listing.** Add Clean Path Credit to your discounts / member-benefits page with a CU-member discount code we provide. The member pays Clean Path directly at the discounted rate; the CU is not in the payment flow.

3. **In-branch leave-behind.** Place this one-pager at member-services counters and lending desks. Tellers and loan officers hand it to declined members.

> **No fees flow between us.** We do not pay the credit union for member referrals, and the credit union does not pay us. Members pay Clean Path Credit directly. Our partner agreement is structured RESPA-safe and Texas-CSO-Act compliant.
