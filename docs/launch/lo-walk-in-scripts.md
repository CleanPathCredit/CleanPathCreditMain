# LO / Real Estate Agent / F&I Walk-In Scripts

Three-channel outreach playbook for Track A4 (Clean Path Credit + Real Estate Agent / LO Referral Pipeline). Scripts are written for an in-person Bexar County motion: walk in with breakfast tacos and a one-pager, leave with a Calendly booking or a denied-file referral.

**IMPORTANT — RESPA Section 8 caveat.** None of these scripts mention dollar amounts paid to the LO/agent for closed loans. Until your attorney has structured the LO compensation model (MSA, fair-market services agreement, or consumer-direct only), do NOT promise a per-closing payment. The scripts use "partnership terms discussed during onboarding" as the placeholder.

---

## 1. Cold-Call Script (45 seconds)

**Use when:** You've identified an LO via Google, NMLS Consumer Access, Zillow, or a brokerage roster, and you're calling their office line during business hours.

```
Hey [First Name], this is Alex with Clean Path Credit here in San Antonio. I'll be quick — are you the LO who handles credit-challenged buyers, or is that someone else on your team?

[If yes / if they listen]

Great. Quick reason for the call: most LOs we partner with were losing 25 to 40 percent of their files to credit denials and didn't have a credit-repair partner they actually trusted to send buyers to. We're a CROA-compliant credit-repair operation that specializes in mortgage readiness — bilingual, San Antonio based, focused on FICO 10T and VantageScore 4.0 prep.

I'd love to drop off a one-pager and a breakfast taco next time I'm in your area, or grab 15 minutes on Calendly to walk you through how the handoff works. Which works better for you?
```

**Objection handling:**

- *"We already have a credit-repair partner."* → "Totally hear that. Most LOs we work with started with someone — usually a generic dispute mill that took 6+ months and didn't communicate back. If you've got 15 min, I can show you what makes our handoff different. Worst case, you've got a backup."

- *"Send me an email."* → "Will do — what's your best email? And while I've got you, do you have a denied or borderline file sitting on your desk this week? I'll do a free credit pre-screen with the borrower so you can see how we work before any commitment."

- *"What does this cost me?"* → "Nothing. The borrower is the client and pays the program fee directly. Partnership terms with you we discuss on the onboarding call — the structure depends on your state and whether you're handling federally-related loans."

## 2. Walk-In Pitch (3 minutes)

**Use when:** You're physically dropping in at an LO office, brokerage, or dealership F&I desk. Bring tacos.

```
Morning, I'm Alex from Clean Path Credit. I'm not here to take much of your time — just dropping off this one-pager and tacos for the team. We help LOs save deals that get denied for credit. If you've ever lost a buyer to a 580 score that needed to be at 620, that's exactly what we fix.

[Hand over the one-pager and the tacos]

Quick question — what's your typical move when a file comes in below threshold? Do you tell them to wait, send them somewhere, or do they just disappear?

[Listen]

Yeah, that's the gap we fill. CROA-compliant, San Antonio based, focused on getting credit-challenged borrowers ready for FICO 10T and VantageScore 4.0 — the new scores Fannie and Freddie are phasing in. Average file we work runs 60 to 90 days from intake to mortgage-ready.

The one-pager's got everything. Calendly link's at the bottom for a 15-minute partnership call when you're ready. And if you've got a denied file this week, send 'em over — first credit pre-screen is free for any borrower you refer.
```

**If they engage and ask follow-up questions:**

- *How is this different from [competitor X]?* → One specialist per file, bilingual, written weekly progress reports the LO gets a copy of, and we focus specifically on mortgage-readiness vs. score-padding. Most generic credit-repair shops just file bulk disputes; we strategy each file.

- *How fast can you get a 580 to a 620?* → Cannot promise. Under CROA we can't guarantee any specific score outcome. What we can tell you: we'll do a free pre-screen on the file and tell you within 48 hours whether we believe we can help, and if so what we think the timeline looks like.

- *Are you licensed?* → We're registered as a Credit Services Organization with the Texas Secretary of State and bonded as required by Texas Finance Code Chapter 393. (Have your CSO registration number and surety bond carrier ready when this question lands. If you're not yet registered, do not walk into LO offices — get registered first.)

## 5. LO Archetype Variations

Same core scripts, but lead with archetype-specific pain.

### Archetype A: Independent Mortgage Broker (1-3 LOs, owner-operator)

**Pain:** Every denied file is a direct hit to the LO's commission — there's no salaried floor. They feel every lost deal personally.

**Lead-in tweak:** *"Most independent brokers we work with are losing $X commission every time a file gets denied for credit. We're the salvage channel for those files. Bilingual, CROA-compliant, San Antonio based — and you keep the relationship with the borrower the whole time."*

**Best book-the-call hook:** "I can do a free pre-screen on any denied file you've got sitting on your desk right now — within 48 hours. Want to send one over?"

### Archetype B: Brokerage LO (UWM, Movement, Guild, Caliber, Fairway, etc.)

**Pain:** Has corporate channel partners, but they're slow, generic, and English-only. Borderline Latino working-class files fall through the cracks.

**Lead-in tweak:** *"Most brokerage LOs already have a corporate credit-repair partner — they're slow, English-only, and not built for borderline Latino working-class files. We fill that specific gap. Bilingual, San Antonio based, fast pre-screens."*

**Best book-the-call hook:** "You don't have to switch from your existing partner. Use us for the borderline Spanish-speaking files your current partner doesn't handle well."

### Archetype C: Dealership F&I Manager (auto, used and franchise)

**Pain:** F&I bumps 25-40% of buyers at the desk for credit. Most just walk and never come back. Cox Automotive 2026: affordability is the #1 dealer concern.

**Lead-in tweak:** *"F&I bumps roughly a third of buyers at the desk. Most of them just walk. We're the recovery channel — 60 to 90 days, mortgage-grade credit-repair, then they come back to you for the auto loan with a score that approves. Bilingual, CROA-compliant."*

**Best book-the-call hook:** "You don't pay anything. The borrower pays the program fee directly. The dealership gets the loan back when they're approval-ready — that's the whole pitch."

## 7. Pre-Walk-In Checklist

Before you walk into the first LO office, confirm:

- [ ] Texas CSO registration filed and approved (Texas Finance Code Chapter 393)
- [ ] $10,000 surety bond placed and on file with the Texas Secretary of State
- [ ] Attorney-reviewed LO compensation structure (RESPA Section 8 cleared)
- [ ] Attorney-reviewed consumer agreement (CROA §405 disclosures, 3-day cancel)
- [ ] Attorney-reviewed fee structure (TSR §310.4(a)(2) — collect after demonstrated results)
- [ ] One-pager printed (50+ copies)
- [ ] Calendly link configured and tested
- [ ] Free pre-screen workflow live (intake form, 48-hour SLA, signed credit-pull authorization)
- [ ] CSO registration number and surety bond carrier name memorized for the "are you licensed?" question
- [ ] Spanish-language consumer disclosure available on request
- [ ] Smoke-tested funnel end-to-end (a real intake-to-onboarding run-through with a real card)

If any of these items is unchecked, **delay the walk-in motion**. The first LO who asks "are you registered?" and gets a no closes the door on every other LO in their network.
